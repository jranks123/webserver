What is a YouTube ID?

To find a YouTube video identifier, please select the alphanumeric code from your video's URL.

It is located after the watch?v= part of the URL.

In the example below, the video identifier would be: YdXFv4wgrgg



YouTube identifiers are alphanumeric and between 10 and 12 characters.
